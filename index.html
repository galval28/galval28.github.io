Lightning Facts

  • Favorite part about programming: Short answer: Data structures. Long answer: Determining the best way to structure data gives me a rush. I am constantly looking for newer, more efficient ways to store, retrieve, and sort data. At work I developed a timeline of connection states, finally finding a good use for a hash map. At first the key to the map was just an id that mapped to which month of data we needed. This way we could only get a months worth of states from the server at a time and allow caching. Then I went even further to make the map a tuple. Now the key, which is the timeline you want, is mapped to the other map with the states. It was really fun to think up.

Future...

Looking forward, I would like to start working on a kind of social media for college students looking for projects. I know that I am always looking for people that have the drive to work on projects with outside of class. I also believe that it can really showcase what companies are looking for in employees.

  1. Experience. So many companies are looking for college students with experience, and it can be difficult in college to get that experience. I believe that this program could make that easier.
  2. Passion. As a college student, it can be difficult to find time to work on personal projects. I think that one of the reasons is that its also hard to find a project that you want to spend your time on. With this program, the goal is that anyone can find a project that interests them, and people to share the work with.
  3. Collaboration. One thing that I have found, is that the computer/software engineering field has become very competitive. It's not enough to just be good at programming anymore, you also need to be able to work closely with others. My program would make it easy for anyone who wants to work on collaborative projects, have that opportunity.

My hope for this project is to get people involved firstly in the making of the project, second in the use of the program farther down the road. I believe that this project could give companies an insight into what students are actually working on besides course work and show that they are able to work in groups. Currently, this is just an idea, but I hope that I can have this project running by the end of this semester.
